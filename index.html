The Book of Memories

Posted on 23 February 2024

The Book of Memories is a student-owned website by Katelyn. In this website, you will hear about the beauty of scrapbooking, and the importance of it. I hope that this website will be able to influence you to start scrapbooking!

The Beauty of Scrapbooking. Scrapbooks are a record of your life from now till the end of each book. You may document your life from special days, birthdays, weddings, vacations, or your everyday life. Your creativity now will be placed into a scrapbook, and you will always feel the pleasure of the hard work you have placed into it.

Items Needed to Start a Scrapbook Amount Price
A scrapbook or a blank book (no lines) 1 $3.00 - $15 (depending on size & quality)
Printed photos (on sticker paper is most recommended) depending on how much photos you want for each page. estimate $2.00-$7.00
Glue (not required if you print on a sticker paper) 1 $4.00-$6.00
Scissors 1 $2.00
